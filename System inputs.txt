System inputs:

-Amount of people (single person or couple)
-Age(s) of the people
-Longevity (based on a simple questionnaire) with a default value
-When they will retire or have retired
-Provide taxable income quantity from 1040 tax form

SOURCES OF INCOME
-Income from social security
	-If you haven't started social security when will you start receiving and how much do you expect?
-Pensions (have they started, how much money, any cost of living adjustments, survivorship benefit)
-Rental income (net after expenses of all properties)
-Projected inheritances, how much and when
-Other income (misc)

CAPITAL
[After tax taxable] 
-In the bank, checkings, savings, money market, CD. (exclude what is considered an "emergency fund" and exclude money set aside for a big purchase in the next 5 years. 
-How much in brokerage account and mutual funds that are not retirement accounts
[Before tax deferred] (Separate spouse answers)
-401k, 403B, 457, IRA, etc
-Approximately how much is the average return of the portfolio over the past 20 years. We can provide the user a few templates (pie chart style) to match with their profile so they don't actually have to look up the returns themselves. 
[After tax tax free] (Separate spouse answers)
-Roth 401k, Roth IRA (another pie chart diagram here again)
